# Loxley Advisory Group

**Modernization Without Volatility**

Loxley Advisory Group is a technology advisory practice serving PE-backed and growth-stage companies. We work at the intersection of technical rigor and business consequence: technical due diligence that informs deal decisions, fractional CTO and CIO leadership for companies navigating transitions, and AI strategy grounded in operating reality rather than vendor roadmaps.

This repository publishes the frameworks that structure our client engagements. They reflect how we think about the work and what clients can expect when they engage us.

---

## Frameworks

### Technical Health Assessment
[`/tha/loxley-tha-framework.html`](./tha/loxley-tha-framework.html)

Our methodology for assessing the technical health of a business in the context of the decision the reader is trying to make. A PE firm pricing a deal, a CEO preparing for a transaction, and a board navigating a leadership change are asking different questions. The framework adapts to each.

Covers an eight-domain diagnostic structure, a composable elective system, five engagement contexts, three depth tiers, and a full deliverable stack including the Integration Velocity Rubric for pre-acquisition diligence.

---

## Engagements

Our work spans six practice areas:

**Technical Due Diligence** for PE firms and acquirers requiring a rigorous, decision-ready view of a target's technology posture before close.

**Fractional CTO and CIO Leadership** for companies that need senior technology leadership on a defined timeline without a permanent hire.

**Interim Technology Executive Placement** when an organization needs leadership continuity while a permanent search is underway.

**Technical Health Assessments** for operating companies, portfolio businesses, and boards who need an honest view of where they stand and what to address first.

**AI Strategy and Readiness** for leadership teams navigating the gap between AI potential and production reality.

**Strategic Viability Planning** for organizations evaluating whether a technology investment, platform bet, or modernization initiative is worth pursuing before capital is committed, or whether an existing product or initiative has earned continued investment. Structured as a composable engagement that produces a clear viability position rather than a hedged recommendation.
