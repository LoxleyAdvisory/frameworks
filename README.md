# Loxley Advisory Group

**Modernization Without Volatility**

Loxley Advisory Group is a technology advisory practice for PE-backed and growth-stage companies. The work spans six service lines: technical due diligence, fractional CTO and CIO leadership, interim technology executive placement, technical health assessments, AI strategy and readiness, and strategic viability planning.

The discipline is in the tagline. Modernization is the goal. Volatility is what we keep out of it.

This repository publishes the frameworks that structure our client engagements.

---

## Frameworks

### Technical Health Assessment
[`/tha/loxley-tha-framework.html`](./tha/loxley-tha-framework.html)

A methodology for assessing technical health in the context of the decision the reader needs to make. A PE firm pricing a deal, a CEO preparing for a transaction, and a board facing a leadership change are asking different questions. The framework adapts to each without diluting the rigor behind it.

Eight domains, a composable elective system, five engagement contexts, three depth tiers, and a full deliverable stack including the Integration Velocity Rubric for pre-acquisition diligence.

---

## Engagements

**Technical Due Diligence** for PE firms and acquirers who need a decision-grade view of a target's technology posture before close.

**Fractional CTO and CIO Leadership** for companies that need senior technology leadership on a defined timeline without a permanent hire.

**Interim Technology Executive Placement** when an organization needs leadership continuity while a permanent search runs.

**Technical Health Assessments** for operating companies, portfolio businesses, and boards that need an honest read on where they stand and what to address first.

**AI Strategy and Readiness** for leadership teams that have invested in AI capability and need to make it deliver. Governance, architecture, and the operational discipline that turn pilots into production.

**Strategic Viability Planning** for organizations weighing whether a technology investment, platform bet, or modernization initiative is worth pursuing before capital is committed, or whether an existing product has earned continued investment. Composable in scope. The output is a clear viability position rather than a hedged recommendation.
