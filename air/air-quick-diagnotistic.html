Dimension 01 Model Autonomy

Dimension 02 Data Liquidity and Feedback Loops
How does the AI system receive the data it needs for inference relative to when the underlying events occur?
How does the organization capture whether AI outputs were correct after the fact?
What governs the relationship between the teams that supply data and the AI systems that consume it?
Dimension 03 Compute and Margin Ratio
How thoroughly have production inference costs been modeled — including peak load, redundancy, and the full inference stack beyond API call costs?
For initiatives designed to displace manual work, how well understood is the actual cost of the manual process being replaced?
At production volume, how does the cost of automation compare to the cost of the process it displaces?
